Facilities inside hostels

Given below are a few facilities that are provided by all the hostels in our institute.

Gymnasium

Fitness is what keeps a person healthy and going.We have to provided the facility of GYM in all our hostels so that students can stay fit.Gym will be open accordind to its timings which you will be informed of in your respective hostels.

Read More
Mess

All the three basic meals of the day(breakfast,lunch,dinner) are being served by the Mess. All the hostels have their own mess.All the mess follow a Menu which change weekly.We make sure that food is made with keeping sanitation in mind.

Read More
Sports facilities

All the hostles have been provided with a table tennis room inside them so that students can play at odd hours of the day.Hostels with lawns also have badminton court.

Read More
TV room

Students need to keep themselves updated be it current affairs,sportsor any other entertainment related stuff.For that we have made sure that all hostels have a common TV room.
